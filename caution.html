Guns don't kill people, people kill people. Please refrain from doing anything stupid with your firearms. Guns are by no means toys, but rather they are TOOLS. Buying a gun for the purpose of fun is a wasted venture, because sooner or later it will bite you in the butt. There have been a few occasions where I failed to remember this, and had I not been following at least a few safety guidelines I, or a member of my family, might very well be dead right now. When it comes to guns accidents result in tragedy. Learn and practice proper gun safety religiously. Here are just a few things to be aware of while performing gun related activities:

  • Never point your gun in an unsafe direction, treat it as if it were loaded at all times
  • Wear proper safety equipment when shooting, eye and ear protection are a good start
  • Clean your gun regularly
  • Store your firearm and ammunition in a secure location, possibly even in seperate locations
  • When reloading be aware of pressure specifications for your firearm and do not surpass them
  • When shooting steel, maintain appropriate distances to reduce the risk of ricochet injury
