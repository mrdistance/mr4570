Welcome to my library of 45-70 load data! Below you will find the various loads that I have gathered from studying reloading manuals. The weights listed are in grains and velocities are in feet per second. Remember, always start 10% lower and work your way up. Maintain C.O.A.L.-Even a slight adjustment between 2.525 to 2.510 can lead to thousands of pounds difference in pressure. Be wary of the type of firearm you are using and understand its maximum operating pressures. Never exceed the maximum load as this can result in injury or death. When testing, monitor for signs of pressure such as difficulty ejecting brass and flattened primers. I want to give a special thanks to Lee Precision for granting me permission to share some of the data in their manual "Modern Reloading" (2nd Edition). This has the most extensive set of load data for the 45-70 I have found. I also use Lee components for all of my reloading tools because they are inexpensive and quality built. Be sure to check them out!
