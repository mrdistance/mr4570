MR 45-70

  • HANDLOADING SUPPLIES

    1. Brass
    2. Primer(Large Rifle)
    3. Powder
    4. Bullet
    5. Load Data

  • RELOADING STEPS

    1. Resize
    2. Deprime
    3. Reprime
    4. Flair & Add Powder
    5. Seat Bullet
    6. Crimp

  • POWDERCOATING SUPPLIES

    1. Powder Coat
    2. Oven(Not Used For Food)
    3. Container for Mixing
    4. Tray
    5. Unsized Bullets

  • POWDERCOATING STEPS

    1. Coat Bullets
    2. Cure for 20 Minutes at 400 degrees
    3. Remove and let Cool
    4. Apply More Coats if Necessary

  • BULLET CASTING SUPPLIES

    1. Lead Melter
    2. Bullet Mold
    3. Ladle(If Not a Bottom Pour Pot
    4. Mallet, Gloves, and Container
    5. Long Clothes and Fresh Air
    6. Lead

  • BULLET CASTING STEPS

    1. Melt the Lead
    2. Heat Up Mold
    3. Fill Mold
    4. Break Sprew
    5. Drop Bullets

  • SUPPRESSOR SUPPLIES

    1. Approved Form 1
    2. Tube
    3. Threaded Endcap
    4. Baffles/Spacers/Monocore

  • SUPPRESSOR BUILDING STEPS

    1. Complete a Form 1
    2. Find Materials for Diameter, Length, Caliber
    3. Ensure Internals Are At Least .06 Over Bullet Diameter
    4. Provide Blast Chamber to Contain Initial Explosion
    5. Balance Size with Practicality
